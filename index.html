Summary

Dr. Anh Nguyen-Duc is a Professor at the University of South Eastern Norway where he leads the Management Information System resarch group. He is one of the leading researchers in the domain of sosftware startups and co-founder of the Software Startup Research Network. He is also a pionner researcher about Generative AI. He publishes a recent book entitled Generative AI for Effective Software Development. Besides his academic endeavors, he also engage in consultancy and startups mentor. His latest involvement in startups include Muml - a hyperlocal news platform and StartupGPT - a virtual assisant for startup founders.
