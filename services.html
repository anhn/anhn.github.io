Our Services

Startup Consultancy

With extensive research and practical experience, I provide consultancy to software startups, helping them navigate challenges from prototyping to market entry. My consultancy covers key areas such as:

  • Prototyping and Product Validation: Guiding startups through the process of turning ideas into prototypes, testing them in the market, and refining based on user feedback
  • Business Development Strategies: Helping startups define their business models, identify market opportunities, and create sustainable growth strategies.

Software Development Consultancy

I offer guidance on software development methodologies, agile practices, and quality assurance based on years of academic research and industrial collaboration. Key aspects of this service include:

  • Agile Methodology Implementation: Offering advice on how to implement agile frameworks, such as Scrum or Kanban, in different organizational contexts to increase team efficiency and product delivery cycles.
  • Software Quality Assurance: Ensuring that software development practices are aligned with industry standards and best practices to enhance quality control, automated testing, and continuous integration.
  • Process Optimization: Helping organizations streamline their software development processes to reduce waste, enhance productivity, and ensure that teams can adapt to changing project requirements.
  • Technical Debt and Refactoring: Assisting organizations in managing technical debt through code refactoring, ensuring long-term maintainability and scalability of their software products

Generative AI Training and Consultancy

  • Generative AI Integration: Assisting companies in exploring and deploying generative AI tools like large language models, image synthesis, and natural language processing to create innovative products and solutions.
  • Generative AI Integration: Assisting companies in exploring and deploying generative AI tools like large language models, image synthesis, and natural language processing to create innovative products and solutions.
  • Workforce Training: Providing specialized training programs to upskill employees on the latest AI and generative AI technologies, helping them understand AI fundamentals, practical implementation, and troubleshooting common AI-related challenges.
